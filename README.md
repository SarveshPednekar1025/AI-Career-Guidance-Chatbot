# AI Career Guidance Chatbot

## Project Overview

AI Career Guidance Chatbot is a chatbot developed using Botpress to help students identify suitable career paths based on their engineering branch, interests, and future goals.

## SDG

SDG 4 – Quality Education

## Features

* Career Recommendations
* Skill Suggestions
* Internship Guidance
* Higher Studies Guidance
* Government Job Guidance
* Entrepreneurship Guidance

## Technology Used

* Botpress
* Artificial Intelligence
* Natural Language Processing (NLP)
* Prompt Engineering

## Users

* Engineering Students
* Diploma Students
* Fresh Graduates

## Future Scope

* Voice Assistant Integration
* Multilingual Support
* Mobile Application
* Advanced AI Recommendations
